Frequently Ask Questions.

Do I have to be of certain age to play NAIRABET Online and to use the NairaBet Mobile ® app?

No. However, you have to be of playable age which is 18years minimum according to the Nigeria Law.You can register Online via your desktop PC via NairaBet.com or via the NAIRABET Mobile® app from your eligible mobile device.

What can I do with my NAIRABET Mobile ® app?
  • Create a NairaBet Account
  • Place Bet on any event of your choice anytime and anywhere.
  • Credit your NairaBet account on the go.
  • Cash out anytime anywhere.

    • Some features are available only for eligible accounts. Restrictions may apply.

What security features are in place to protect my account information on my Phone?

With our Moble App ®, All your personal and Financial data are secure as we made use 128-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt your personal information such as User IDs, Passwords and account information. Our Mobile ® app will decode any encrypted information we send you. We also use multifactor authentication that verifies that you own the accounts you want to access when you first log in using the NairaBet Mobile® app. To do this, you'll need to request an Identification Code, which you can receive by email, phone or text message.
